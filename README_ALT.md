## The Short Version

I am a **Senior Backend Software Engineer** with **12+ years** building software systems, APIs, business workflows, and products.

My work is focused on one clear direction:

> Turn real-world problems into software products using backend engineering, software architecture, automation, and applied AI.

I am not building a profile around tutorials, experiments, or disconnected demos. I am building an ecosystem of products under **Trevvos Soluções em IA**, where backend architecture and AI are used as practical tools for decision-making, productivity, and business operations.
